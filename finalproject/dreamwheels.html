Dream Wheels - Premium Car Showcase

Reason for Name Selection: The name Dream Wheels reflects the website’s focus on high-performance, luxury, and aspirational cars. “Dream” conveys the idea of vehicles that many enthusiasts aspire to own, while “Wheels” is a casual, recognizable term for cars. Together, the name is short, memorable, and directly connected to the project’s purpose — showcasing premium vehicles that inspire passion and admiration.

Site Purpose

Dream Wheels serves as an interactive hub for luxury and high-performance car enthusiasts. The site will provide detailed information on a wide range of premium vehicles, including specifications, images, pricing, and availability. Users will be able to browse through dynamically generated car listings, view in-depth details through modals, and submit inquiries via a contact form. The purpose of the site is to inform and engage visitors who are passionate about cars, helping them explore and compare models while delivering a smooth, responsive, and accessible browsing experience.

Scenarios

  • Which luxury car models are currently available, and what are their prices?
  • What are the detailed specifications and features of the BMW X6M compared to Porsche Cayenne turbo S?
